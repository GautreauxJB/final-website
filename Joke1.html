The Programmer’s Calendar Problem

A programmer spent all night debugging his calendar app because he couldn’t figure out why it kept mixing up holidays. He finally realized that for him, October 31 and December 25 weren’t two different days—they were the same. Confused? That’s because in programming, Octal 31 equals Decimal 25. He laughed, his cat judged him, and his coworkers still don’t know why he’s handing out candy and fruitcake at the same time every year.
