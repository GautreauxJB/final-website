IT Support’s Light Bulb Dilemma

The office light went out, so someone called IT. When asked how many IT staff it would take to change it, the intern shrugged. “Probably none,” said the IT guy, “we’ll just tell everyone to turn it off and on again and hope the problem fixes itself.” By the end of the day, the lights were still out, but at least everyone got a lot of practice pressing the switch—because in IT, rebooting is always the first solution, even if it doesn’t work.
