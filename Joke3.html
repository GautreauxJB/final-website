The Password Crisis

An employee called IT crying because she couldn’t log in. The IT guy asked, “What’s the password you’re using?” She whispered, “Password123.” He facepalmed. “No, that won’t work—it’s case sensitive.” She sighed, “I knew that. But I didn’t know it was emotionally sensitive too!” After twenty minutes of trying, she finally reset her password, proving once and for all that in IT, even passwords have feelings.
